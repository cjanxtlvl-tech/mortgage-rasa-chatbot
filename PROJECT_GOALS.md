# Project Goals: Mortgage Lead Capture Chatbot

## Business Objective
This chatbot supports a mortgage lead generation website by helping convert website visitors into qualified leads while improving engagement.

Primary goals:
- Capture mortgage leads directly from website conversations
- Improve visitor engagement and reduce drop-off during early research
- Guide visitors to the right loan category for their scenario
- Collect contact information for sales follow-up
- Support smooth handoff to human follow-up for next steps

## Supported Loan Categories
The assistant should guide users across these categories:
- Purchase
- Refinance
- Government
- Jumbo
- Investment
- Hard Money
- SBA
- Commercial

## Customer Experience Intent
The chatbot should:
- Respond quickly to common mortgage questions
- Offer clear next steps without overwhelming users
- Collect practical pre-qualification details at a light level
- Offer callback and consultation options when users are ready

## What The Chatbot Should Not Do
The chatbot is not a loan officer or underwriter. It must not:
- Guarantee approvals
- Quote official or final rates
- Make underwriting decisions
- Provide tax advice or legal advice

## Business Outcome Expectations (V1)
- Increase number of captured leads from website traffic
- Improve lead quality with basic pre-qualification context
- Shorten response time from first visit to first contact
- Improve handoff quality for human loan specialists
