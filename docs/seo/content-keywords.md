# Content And Keyword Planning

## Primary Service Keyword Groups
- Mortgage broker services
- Home purchase loans
- Refinance services
- Pre-approval and pre-qualification
- FHA, VA, conventional, jumbo loan options

## Informational Keyword Groups
- Mortgage process explainers
- Credit score and qualification guidance
- Down payment and closing cost education
- Rate lock and mortgage term education

## Transactional Keyword Groups
- Apply for mortgage online
- Get pre-approved now
- Speak with mortgage advisor
- Request refinance quote

## Comparison Keywords
- FHA vs conventional loan
- Fixed vs adjustable mortgage
- Mortgage broker vs bank lender
- Refinance vs home equity loan

## Local Intent Keywords
- Mortgage broker in [city]
- Home loan advisor [city]
- First-time homebuyer mortgage [city]
- Refinance specialist [city]

## FAQ And Content Support Topics
- How long does mortgage pre-approval take?
- What documents are needed to apply?
- How much house can I afford?
- When does refinancing make financial sense?
- What loan program fits my scenario?

## Page Type Mapping
- Service pages: product-specific transactional intent
- Location pages: local commercial intent
- FAQ pages: informational support with internal links to money pages
- Comparison pages: mid-funnel decision support
- Blog updates: market insights and evergreen explainers

## Example Keyword Mapping Table
| Keyword Theme | User Intent | Recommended Page Type | Conversion Goal |
